About Me

I'm an MSc student at the University of Edinburgh studying High Performance Computing. I have many interests which include compilers, computer architecture, machine learning systems, affective computing, theory of computation, hardware, algorithms, robotics, distributed systems, P vs NP, HPC, data science, concurrent computing (I also love physics, maths & philosophy). I'm currently working on a paper that examines the performance of two implementations of a parallel adaptive quadrature algorithm using OpenMP.
