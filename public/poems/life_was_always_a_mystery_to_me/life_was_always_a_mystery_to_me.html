Life Was Always A Mystery To Me

Life was always a mystery to me.
I never really figured out how to navigate this maze since I was a tiny seed

Life has always been so cold to me
I can feel myself freezing from head to toe as time proceeds

Life always felt like one of those puzzles, where pieces seem to change after it has finally been assembled

I keep living in my imagination because reality isn’t what it seems
I keep calling myself a new name every year to shake the fact that I’m growing old
I keep trying to walk my way through this den in which the lions have been let loose
I keep occupying myself with thoughts of a distant future that may never come true
I keep looking for love in places that don’t bear fruit
I keep trying to be kind as a way to find inner peace
I keep trying all these things while life keeps trying me
I keep running through so many paths; I guess I’ve reached the end of my trip

I guess life was never meant for me
I guess my heart was too pure for a world full of sin
I guess life was forever going to leave me in between
I guess life was always going to be like a movie, which I never got to see

We are all just strings in a melody
We are still all just seeds to a tree
We are all just underwater in this sea
We are all what we are; some say we are even what we eat
I guess I ate death cause this feels like purgatory
